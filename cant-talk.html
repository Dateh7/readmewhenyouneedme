When You Want to Talk but Don’t Know How

I know it's not always easy for you to talk about how you're feeling. And that’s okay.

You don’t need to have all the words. You don’t need to explain things perfectly. The fact that you're even trying — that you want to share — means so much to me.

I will always be patient with you. I won't push. I won't rush you. I'm here to hold space for whatever you’re feeling, even if it comes out in pieces or pauses.

You don’t have to carry everything by yourself. You can take your time. You can come to me when you're ready. And even in the silence — I’m listening, I’m present, and I’m with you.

I appreciate your heart, your effort, and your courage. You're never alone in this.
