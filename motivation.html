When You Need Motivation

Hey you. I know some days feel heavier than others. You wake up, look at everything ahead of you, and just think, "How am I supposed to do all of this?"

But I need you to remember something, you are one of the most capable, focused, determined people I know. You’ve overcome things before that were harder than this. You’ve kept going even when no one else saw the struggle. And look at you now.

Whatever you're working toward, be it school, your dreams, your healing, or just making it through the day. I’m proud of you. Always.

You don’t need to be perfect. You just need to keep going. Rest if you have to. Cry if you need. But don’t ever doubt what you’re made of.

You’ve got this. And I’ve got you.

Small steps. Big heart.
