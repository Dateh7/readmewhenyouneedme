When we haven't spoken in a while

Even if we’re not speaking... my heart hasn’t gone anywhere.

If we haven’t spoken in a while, and you're here reading this — just know, I’ve been thinking about you. I miss you. And no matter the silence, I still care about you deeply.

If I’m the reason for the distance... I’m truly sorry. I never want to be the one who makes you feel alone or unsure. If I needed the space — it’s okay. I understand. And whatever it is I'll forgive you. Just give me a little time, and I’ll find my way back to you.

We’re not perfect — but we are real. And even in the quiet, I haven’t and will never stop loving you. My heart has a spot only you can fill, and no silence can ever change that.

So when you're ready, come back to me — even if it's just with a “hey.” I’ll be here. Still yours. Always.
