When You Need to Laugh

Your laugh is contagious — and unfairly cute

7–0. Sunderland vs Real Madrid. Iconic.

If you're here, it means you're in need of a good laugh And while you're usually the reason I'm smiling, let’s be real lowkey — **I'm the funny one.**

I mean... remember the time I beat you 7–0 in FIFA? You had Real Madrid. I had Sunderland. You didn’t think it was funny — but that only makes it funnier.

The truth is, some of my favorite moments are the ones where we’re laughing, but we forget why. Your laugh is one of my favorite sounds — especially when I’m the reason for it.

So if your cheeks aren’t hurting yet, just picture me talking about your FIFA skills cause that would crack me up... Or better yet, just come back to me — I’ll have better jokes ready.
