WWhen you're excited about something

Your joy looks really, really good on you

Whatever it is — big, small, silly, serious — I really want to hear about it. I want to see your eyes light up. I want to hear you yap and smile wide.

Your joy is contagious. And honestly? It’s one of my favourite things about you. When you’re excited, the world feels lighter, brighter and better.

So please don’t ever feel like you’re “too much” for being excited. Your passions, your wins, your weird little obsessions (matcha, even though I don't get it) — I love all of it.

Whether you aced a test, found a cute top at Zara, or just had a good cup of coffee — tell me. I want to be the first person you run to. I want to celebrate with you and celebrate you, every time.

Because seeing you happy? That’s my favourite thing in the world.
