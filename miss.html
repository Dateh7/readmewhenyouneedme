When You Miss Me

You never really have to miss me — I'm always with you

If you're reading this, it means your heart feels a little too quiet. And I want you to know — I'm thinking of you too. Missing someone like you is proof of how lucky I am to love you. Whether we're apart for an hour or a day, you are never far from me.

Ever since our first sleepover, I’ve been missing you every single day. Not the kind of missing that hurts — but the kind that reminds me how safe and whole I feel when you’re around. Your absence only makes my love louder.

So when you miss me, just remember: I’m somewhere smiling because of you. And the next time I hold you, I’ll make up for every second we were apart.
