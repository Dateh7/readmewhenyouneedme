When You're Overthinking

If you're overthinking about us — let me remind you: I'm not going anywhere. I'm here. Always. I don’t say that lightly. You don’t need to doubt my heart, or my commitment. I chose you. I keep choosing you. Every day.

If you're overthinking about school — stop and breathe for a moment. You are one of the hardest workers I have ever met. You push through everything with strength, even when you doubt yourself. No exam or assignment is bigger than your ambition. You are built for excellence. You just need to trust your preparation, and trust yourself.

When the world gets loud and your thoughts start to spiral, pause — and remember who you are. You’re more capable than you think. You’ve proven that time and time again. And no matter what, I believe in you. Always.

You’ve got this — always.
