When You're Angry

Heres little Dateh, cause its better to be cool then mad.

So… you’re angry.
Maybe at someone else. Maybe at life. Maybe (and I’m hoping not)… at me?

I don’t really know what “angry you” looks like yet — and that’s probably a good thing. But when that day comes (and I know it will), I’ll be right here. Not to fight. Not to fix everything. Just to be there for you.

I might not always get it right, but I’ll always try to listen, to understand.

And until then — take a deep breath, drink some water, and maybe throw a pillow instead of your phone I love you, even when you're mad.
